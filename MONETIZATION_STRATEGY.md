## Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Risks

**1. Competitive Risk (Medium):**
- GitHub/Microsoft could bundle similar features
- OpenAI/Anthropic could build native tools
- **Mitigation:** Move fast, build community, focus on UX

**2. Pricing Risk (Low):**
- Users may resist paying for "free" tools
- **Mitigation:** Generous free tier, clear value ladder, freemium model

**3. Technical Risk (Low):**
- Browser limitations for complex operations
- **Mitigation:** Progressive enhancement, optional cloud features

**4. Market Risk (Medium):**
- AI hype cycle could cool
- **Mitigation:** Focus on real developer pain points, not trends

**5. Regulatory Risk (Low):**
- Data privacy regulations
- **Mitigation:** Client-side processing, no data collection by default

### Mitigation Strategies

✅ **Diversify revenue streams** (SaaS + marketplace + enterprise)  
✅ **Build defensible moats** (community, integrations, compliance)  
✅ **Maintain low burn rate** (client-side = minimal infrastructure)  
✅ **Focus on retention** (churn < 5% monthly)  
✅ **Expand use cases** (more tools in suite)

What makes a prompt "good"?

A great prompt tells the AI who it is, what to do, what format to use, and any rules to follow — all in one message. That's called a one-shot prompt, and it's what separates people who get great AI results from those who don't.

Every Prompt Studio template is pre-built with all four ingredients — just fill in your specific details and the AI gets everything it needs before you even hit send.

❓ FAQ

Common questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before you start.

Do I need to know how to write prompts?
No. That's exactly why Prompt Studio exists. Pick a template, fill in the blanks, and you have a complete, well-structured prompt — no experience required.
What AI models does it work with?
Prompt Studio generates prompts you can copy and paste into ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Pro users can also test prompts directly against GPT-4o, Claude 3, and Gemini inside the studio — without leaving the page.
What's the difference between Free and Pro?
The Free plan gives you 1 template (you pick which one), the editor, variables, and copy-to-clipboard export. Pro ($9/mo) unlocks all 21 templates, voice input, AI model testing, unlimited version history, share via URL, and MCP server access.
Do I need a credit card to start?
No. The free plan requires only an email address — no credit card, no trial period, no expiry. Upgrade to Pro or Team whenever you're ready.
What is a prompt template?
A template is a pre-written prompt with fill-in-the-blank fields (called variables). For example, a Code Review template has fields for language, code, and issue type. You fill them in, and the studio assembles a complete, ready-to-use prompt automatically.
What is voice input and how does it work?
Voice input lets you dictate your prompt out loud instead of typing. Click the 🎙️ mic button, speak naturally, and your words appear in real time — transcribed by your browser's built-in speech recognition. No plugins, no setup. Works in 40+ languages. Voice input is a Pro feature.
What is MCP and do I need it?
MCP (Model Context Protocol) lets developers connect Prompt Studio to AI coding tools like Goose, Claude Desktop, and Cursor — so your saved templates become callable tools inside your workflow. If you're not a developer, you don't need MCP. It's a Pro+ feature.
Can I share prompts with my team?
Yes. Pro users can share any prompt as a URL — teammates open the link and see the full template, variables, and preview instantly, with no account required to view. The Team plan ($29/mo) adds a shared library, custom template builder, locked variable defaults, and team version history.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your prompts are private by default — only you can see them. Sharing is always opt-in. Voice input is processed entirely in your browser and never sent to our servers. Read the full Privacy Policy.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — cancel anytime from your account settings. No questions asked. Your free plan remains active after cancellation with your 1 selected template intact.
